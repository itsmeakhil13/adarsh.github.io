About Adarsh AS

Adarsh AS is a highly experienced and dedicated legal professional committed to providing exceptional legal services. With a profound understanding of the Indian legal system, Adarsh offers expert guidance and representation across various domains of law. He is an alumnus of Govt Law College Thrissur and practices as a lawyer in the High Court of Kerala.

His practice is built on the principles of integrity, meticulous research, and a client-centric approach. Adarsh believes in delivering clear, practical, and effective legal solutions tailored to the unique needs of each client, ensuring their rights and interests are vigorously protected.

Whether you are navigating complex criminal charges, intricate civil disputes, or seeking proactive legal advice for your personal or business matters, Adarsh AS is your trusted partner for reliable and results-driven legal assistance.

Our Expertise & Practice Areas

Criminal Law

Providing robust defense and legal counsel for various criminal charges, including bail applications, anticipatory bail, trials, appeals, and revision petitions. We handle cases related to IPC, NDPS Act, POCSO Act, and other special laws.

Civil Litigation

Expert representation in civil disputes, including property matters, contract disputes, family law, succession, injunctions, and recovery suits. We represent clients in various civil courts and tribunals.

Legal Consultancy

Proactive legal advice for individuals and businesses on compliance, contracts, property registration, legal notices, and general legal opinions. We assist in drafting legal documents and agreements.
